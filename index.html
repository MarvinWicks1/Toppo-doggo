Meet Alex

✓One-to-one walks and care tailored to each pet's needs.
✓Free meet and greet before the first booking.
✓Designed around trust, comfort and treating pets like family.

“Honey loves Alex coming round to take her for a walk and would recommend her.”

Hi, my name's Alex. I'm starting small but unbelievably proud to be introducing my dog walking and pet services business, Toppo Doggo.

My number one core value is to treat your pets like the family members that they are.

I've been taking care of family and friends' pets for years and, after finally having the courage to leave my NHS job of over a decade, I would like to realise my dream of working with animals.

My services provide one-to-one walks and care tailored to your pet's needs. To do this, I offer a free meet and greet upon your first booking to get to know you and your pet.

I look forward to meeting you all on this new venture.
