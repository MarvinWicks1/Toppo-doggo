Why I started

I’m starting small but I’m unbelievably proud to be introducing my dog walking and pet services business, Toppo Doggo.

I’ve taken care of family and friends’ pets for years. After leaving my NHS job of over a decade, I decided to finally have the courage to work with animals properly.

My number one value is treating your pets like family. I know how much trust it takes to let someone look after them, so I keep things simple, honest and focused on what your pet needs.

Before your first booking, I offer a free meet and greet so I can get to know you and your pet.
